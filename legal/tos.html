All content hosted on /Feds is subject to removal under certain conditions, including but not limited to violations of our policies or the issuance of a court order in accordance with applicable laws. /Feds reserves the right, at its sole discretion, to remove any content for any reason that violates this policy.

HOA Content Removal

The following HOA content can be removed:

  • Links to explicit material involving minors

User Information

/Feds only collects information that users willingly submit to their profiles, such as profile pictures, banners, music, and social links. No additional user data is collected, tracked, or stored.

Law Enforcement Requests

/Feds responds to law enforcement requests only with valid legal documentation. Since we only store voluntarily submitted profile content, only that information is accessible.
Requests should be sent via email to admin@feds.top.
