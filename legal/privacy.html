Information We Collect

Feds only collects information that is willingly provided by users for their profiles. This includes profile pictures (pfps), music preferences, social links, banners, and other optional profile content. No other user information is collected or stored.

Use of Information

The information collected is used solely to display and manage user profiles on the Feds platform. We do not share, sell, or use this information for advertising or tracking purposes.

Law Enforcement Requests

/Feds will respond to law enforcement requests when accompanied by valid legal documentation. Since we do not retain private user data beyond what is voluntarily submitted for profiles, we can only provide access to information that is publicly available or willingly submitted.
Requests should be submitted via email to admin@feds.top for processing.

Your Privacy Rights

Users have the right to manage their own profile content, including removing or editing any information they have voluntarily submitted. Feds does not track activity outside of content explicitly provided to us.
